Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install anything?+
No. Java Vault is a web app that works on any phone, tablet or computer. Just open your browser and go. No app store, no downloads, no updates to manage.
Is my data safe?+
Yes. Your data is protected with password encryption, brute-force lockout after 5 failed attempts, and automatic session timeout. You can also backup and restore all your data at any time.
Can I cancel any time?+
Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel any time from your account settings and you will not be charged again. Your data is yours to export before you leave.
Does it work without internet?+
Most features work offline. Your data is saved locally on your device. The AI Estimate Assistant and shareable invoice links require an internet connection. Everything else works anywhere.
What trades does it support?+
Java Vault is built for all trades including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, roofing, painting, carpentry and general contracting. The AI assistant is trained on pricing for all these trades.
How does the 7-day trial work?+
You get full access to all Pro features for 7 days. No credit card required to start. At the end of the trial you choose a plan or your account moves to a limited free version.
